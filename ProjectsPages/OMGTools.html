Embarking on a journey inspired by Ankama's "One More Gate: A Wakfu Legend," I delved into the intricacies of game development, particularly intrigued by how a small team of two developers could craft such a comprehensive experience.
This curiosity led me to create tools aimed at streamlining the development process.

My initial focus was on a card creation and modification tool.
This tool allows for the design and adjustment of in-game cards, providing flexibility in gameplay mechanics and facilitating rapid prototyping.

Building upon this foundation, I began developing the combat loop, the core system that governs player interactions and battles within the game.

WIP.

These tools are designed to enhance efficiency and creativity in game development, offering a streamlined approach to crafting complex game elements.
By sharing this project, I aim to contribute to the broader game development community, providing resources that can assist others in their creative endeavors.
