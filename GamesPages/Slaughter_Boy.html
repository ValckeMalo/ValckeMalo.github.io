Slaughter Boy is an action-packed rogue-like game that plunges you into a chaotic world where survival is the only option.
Waves of enemies relentlessly pursue you, and the environment constantly shifts, creating a dynamic battlefield filled with danger.
As a lone warrior, you must rise against these overwhelming odds, battling foes and unlocking upgrades to become more powerful and ensure your survival.

Drawing inspiration from the fast-paced gameplay of Brotato, Slaughter Boy offers an exciting blend of rapid combat, character progression, and strategic decision-making.

Whether you choose to fight alone or experiment with different character builds, the game keeps the action intense and fast, with each wave offering new challenges and enemies to face.

With each encounter, you’ll grow stronger, collecting upgrades and unlocking new characters who offer unique abilities to help you battle the increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

The game features :

Survival Mode, where you must endure 20 relentless waves of enemies, each more challenging than the last. Strengthen your skills and gear as you fight to survive until the final wave.

Shop, where you can enhance your character by purchasing powerful upgrades, unlocking new abilities, and customizing your playstyle to dominate the battlefield.

Developed with C# and Unity, Slaughter Boy is built for mobile devices, offering a seamless experience on your phone.
Created by a dedicated team of six programmers and four artists, the game provides an exciting, fast-paced adventure on the go.

Prepare yourself for a thrilling experience filled with intense action, upgrades, and the fight for survival in a world that never stops challenging you.
