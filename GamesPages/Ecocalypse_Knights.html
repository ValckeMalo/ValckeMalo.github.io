Ecocalypse Knight is an action-packed beat-'em-up game that plunges you into a devastated world on the brink of collapse.
A mysterious curse has swept through the land, corrupting nature itself and turning once-beautiful landscapes into apocalyptic battlefields.
As a brave knight, you must rise against this malevolent force, battling relentless waves of corrupted foes and formidable bosses to restore balance to the world.

Drawing inspiration from the beloved Castle Crashers,Ecocalypse Knight offers an exciting blend of fast-paced combat, vibrant visuals, and cooperative chaos.

Whether you choose to embark on this journey alone or alongside friends, the game supports up to four players, both locally and online, using controllers.

With each encounter, you'll grow stronger, enhancing your abilities and unlocking upgrades through a mysterious merchant who aids you on your perilous quest.

The game features two main modes:

Story Mode, where you and your companions must unite your strength and skills to vanquish the curse and its powerful final boss.

Arena Mode, where the bonds of friendship are tested as you face off against one another in fierce combat.

Developed with care by a passionate team of six programmers and four artists, Ecocalypse Knight is built in C++ with SFML, designed to run seamlessly on PC (Windows).

Prepare yourself for a thrilling adventure filled with danger, teamwork, and the fight to reclaim a world lost to darkness.

AI Programmer:

Monsters: Designed and implemented 5 different types of monsters, each featuring unique behaviors and attack patterns to create diverse and engaging combat scenarios.

Bosses: Developed 2 bosses with distinct combat phases, requiring players to adapt their strategies and tactics for each phase.

Gameplay/UI Programmer:

Character Upgrade Menu: Designed and implemented a dynamic and responsive character upgrade menu, seamlessly integrating the upgrades into the game.

Tools Programmer:

Monster Generation Tool: Developed a C# Windows Forms tool to streamline the creation and integration of monsters into the game.
