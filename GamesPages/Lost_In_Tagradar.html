Lost in Tagradar is an immersive simulation game that plunges you into the heart of an uncharted planet, where survival hinges on your ability to harness the power of automation and industry. Stranded on the alien world of Tagradar, you must explore vast landscapes, gather resources, and build complex factories to unlock the secrets of the planet and secure your place within it.

Whether you're constructing massive industrial complexes or fine-tuning your resource management systems, every step you take brings new challenges and opportunities. The more you optimize, the more you'll unlock—enabling you to push the limits of what's possible in this endless, evolving world.

Inspired by the beloved Satisfactory, Lost in Tagradar offers a rich, open-world experience where exploration and optimization go hand in hand.

As you delve deeper into Tagradar’s unknown frontiers, you’ll design intricate production lines, automate processes, and discover innovative solutions to make your factories run more efficiently.

With stunning visuals and seamless gameplay powered by Unity and C#, Lost in Tagradar delivers a smooth, engaging experience that lets you focus on what matters most: building, automating, and surviving.

The game features two key elements:

Exploration Mode, where you venture across Tagradar’s vast landscapes, gather resources, and unlock new technologies to improve your factories and systems.

Optimization Mode, where you focus on refining and expanding your automated processes to create the most efficient and effective factories possible.

Developed by a dedicated team of seven programmers and four talented artists, Lost in Tagradar is built to run smoothly on PC, offering hours of rewarding gameplay as you transform an untamed world into a powerhouse of automation.

Get ready to dive into a world where innovation and exploration are the keys to survival. Lost in Tagradar is waiting for you.

Gameplay Developer:

Conveyor Belts: Designed and implemented conveyor belts using a mathematical algorithm to generate constant curves. Managed the transportation of objects and optimized the system's fluidity and performance.

Optimization: Focused on optimizing game processes, reducing computational costs, and managing performance in large scenes with numerous objects.

Tools Developer:

Creation Tools: Developed four dedicated tools to streamline the creation and integration of objects, milestones, recipes, and building structures within the game.

Graphic Brush Tool: Designed and developed a brush tool for the artists, allowing them to generate objects directly onto the game map.

Sound Developer:

Audio Integration: Integrated sound effects and music into Unity, enhancing the overall audio experience of the game.
