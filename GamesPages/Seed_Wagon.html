SeedWagon invites you to set foot on a mysterious island, caressed by breezes and the song of birds.
A peaceful haven where every step becomes a discovery: lush forests, silver-sand beaches, misty hills.
Everywhere, rare seeds lie waiting to be unearthed and planted to reveal their magic.
Here, exploration is synonymous with wonder, and each corner of the island offers a new spectacle to behold.

Inspired by the soft visuals of Caravan Sandwich and the adventurous spirit of Hover the Hill, SeedWagon offers a contemplative journey underpinned by clean graphics and soothing mechanics.

Built in Unreal Engine 5 with C++ and Blueprints, the game delivers fluid animations,
shimmering lighting, and an open world where flora and fauna evolve in real time.
You’ll wander at your own pace, letting the wind guide you toward the island’s hidden secrets.

At the heart of SeedWagon lies a living gardening cycle: dig up a seed, plant it in the right spot, watch it sprout, and harvest its bounty.
Each plant follows its own life cycle and boasts a unique visual flourish—some burst into spectacular blooms, others grow twisting vines. But your seedlings are more than just beautiful: they activate ancient underground mechanisms.
Place the correct seed on a sacred altar, and hidden doors open, secret passages unfold, and millennia-old puzzles awaken.

SeedWagon is a PC-exclusive title born from the passionate work of seven programmers and six artists.
Every element of this world has been meticulously crafted to inspire contemplation and relaxation.
There’s no rush, no countdown—just the pleasure of watching the island flourish under your touch.
Let yourself be carried away by the gentleness of the environment, listen to nature’s symphony, and bring every corner of this enchanting world to bloom.

Lead Programmer

As one of the technical maestros of the project, I shaped the interactive foundations that elevate SeedWagon:

• Seed Event-Driven Gameplay
I designed and coded the dynamic event system tied to rare seeds: from their discovery to the triggering of magical phenomena, each seed reacts according to its own cycle, unveiling fleeting bridges and visual surprises.

• Linear Structures
I implemented the dynamic event system for various structures, enabling the discovery of remote locations or hidden seeds scattered across the island.

• Mini-Cinematics
To immerse the player in every revelation, I developed brief cinematics integrated directly into the engine, orchestrating camera movements and Blueprint animations to underscore key moments.

• Vehicle Upgrade Interface
I conceived and built a dedicated screen entirely : an upgrade menu for the Wagon-Shuttle where players choose components, boosts, and decorations. Smooth navigation, instant visual feedback, and seamless transitions ensure a clear and rewarding progression.
